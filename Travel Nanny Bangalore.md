**FAQs**

**What is a Travel Nanny or Vacation Babysitter?**  
 A Travel Nanny is a trained caregiver who travels with your family to provide safe and engaging care. They manage routines, meals, hygiene, play, and emotional support during trips.

**What kind of accommodation does the Nanny need?**  
 It depends on your trip and duration. Many parents provide a private room, and some share spaces. Safety, hygiene, and comfort are always ensured.

**What are the boundaries of a Travel Nanny’s responsibilities?**  
 They focus on childcare—supervision, hygiene, meals, play, and travel safety. They do not handle unrelated errands, heavy luggage, or non-prescribed medication.

 **Do I need to cover travel expenses for the Nanny?**

Yes, travel costs (flight/train tickets, accommodation, and meals) for the Nanny are typically covered by the family, in addition to the service fee.

 

**Can the Nanny prepare meals for my child during travel?**

Yes, Travel Nannies can prepare simple meals and snacks as per your instructions, whether at hotels, in transit, or at your accommodation.
