**Frequently Asked Questions about Nest**

**Where is the Nest domestic worker hostel located?**  
Our helper hostel is closer to HSR Layout, Electronic City, and Sarjapur Road in Bangalore and is easy to reach by public transport.

**Is this really a women-only hostel?**  
Yes\! Nest is 100% women-only. Only female residents and authorized female staff are allowed.

**Are meals included in the price?**  
Yes\! All stays include three fresh meals every day—breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

**What type of food do you serve?**  
We serve simple, healthy, home-cooked vegetarian meals, prepared hygienically and suitable for everyone.

**Can I book for just one day?**  
Yes\! You can stay for as little as one day. Short-term stays are welcome.

**Do you accept helpers from all states?**  
Yes. We welcome helpers from all over India. Helpers from Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Jharkhand, Assam, Odisha, West Bengal, Karnataka, and other states regularly stay with us.

**Do I need to pay an advance?**  
We do not charge any advance from either the helper or the customer. 

**What if I don’t have a job yet?**  
No problem. EzyHelpers will train you, give you a certificate, and help you get a good job with a good family and salary.

**Can I cook my own food?**  
Yes\! You can take all three meals from us or cook your own food using our provided utensils. If you choose to cook, you need to bring your own groceries.

**Can I book accommodation for my maid in advance?**  
Yes\! You can book a bed for your helper/maid in advance for any date. This ensures that she has a confirmed place to stay.

**How is Nest different from other maid accommodations or hostels?**  
Nest is made just for domestic workers/helpers. We provide safe rooms, nutritious meals, skill training, and job support. Unlike other hostels, you don’t need to pay any advance for the stay.

**Is there any limit on how long a helper/maid can stay?**  
Helpers/maids can stay for a maximum period of one month.

**Are there any additional costs apart from the stay and meals?**  
No hidden costs. All basic amenities, meals, and facilities are included in the plan you choose.

**Can I extend the stay if my travel plans change?**  
Yes\! You can easily extend your maid/helper’s stay. Just let us know in advance, and we’ll adjust the booking.
