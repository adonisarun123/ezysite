## **Caretaker vs Caregiver: What's the Difference?**

In India, the terms "caretaker" and "caregiver" are used interchangeably. Both refer to a trained, non-medical care professional who provides daily personal support — hygiene, feeding, mobility assistance, medication reminders, and companionship — for a patient or elderly person at home. Some service providers prefer one term over the other, but the role and responsibilities are essentially the same. At EzyHelpers, you can call them caretakers or caregivers. We provide the same verified professionals either way.

A caretaker is different from a [home nurse](/care-services/home-nursing-services-bangalore/) (who handles medical procedures) and different from a [trained attendant](/care-services/trained-attendant-services-bangalore/) (who specialises in high-dependency physical handling such as bedridden or post-spinal care).

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

### **What's the difference between a caretaker and a caregiver?**

In India, the terms are used interchangeably to describe a non-medical care professional who provides daily personal support at home. EzyHelpers uses both terms — the role and responsibilities are the same.

### **What's the difference between a caretaker and a home nurse?**

A caretaker provides non-medical daily support — hygiene, feeding, mobility, companionship, medication reminders. A [home nurse](/care-services/home-nursing-services-bangalore/) is medically trained and handles procedures like wound care, IV therapy, catheter management, and medication administration. Many families combine both for complete care.

### **How quickly can you arrange a caretaker in Bangalore?**

For most areas in Bangalore, we attempt same-day placement based on your specific care needs and availability within our network. Once you reach out, our team begins shortlisting suitable profiles and matching you with the right fit as quickly as possible. The exact timeline may vary depending on your requirements, but we keep you informed at every step.

### **How are your caretakers verified before being placed?**

Every caretaker goes through Aadhaar verification, government ID validation, and background verification, along with a face-to-face interview with our placement team. We carefully assess both their caregiving skills and their behaviour to ensure they are reliable and suitable for your home.

### **What if the caretaker is not a good fit for our family?**

We offer a free replacement if the placement does not work out for any reason. You can raise the concern at any point, and our team will begin the process of finding a better match. We stay involved until you have a caretaker you are genuinely comfortable with.

### **Do you offer live-in and full-time caretaker options?**

Yes. We provide live-in caretakers who stay at the patient's home round-the-clock, and full-time caretakers for complete 8 to 10 hours of daily shifts. Our team can help you determine what fits best.

### **Is there a minimum duration I need to commit to?**

No. We do not work on fixed contracts or minimum durations. You can use the service for a short recovery period or arrange ongoing long-term care. The arrangement is entirely based on your family's needs.

### **Is there a registration or placement fee?**

There is a one-time placement fee, which our team explains in full before any commitment is made. There are no hidden charges added after placement.

### **Can I combine a caretaker with a home nurse for the same patient?**

Yes. This is a common arrangement when a patient needs both medical procedures and daily personal care. The nurse handles medical tasks; the caretaker manages daily needs. We coordinate both placements.
