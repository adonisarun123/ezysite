## **When You Need a Trained Attendant (Not Just a Home Attendant)**

Choose a trained attendant when:

- The patient is **bedridden** and needs scheduled repositioning, hygiene management, and pressure-sore prevention
- The patient has had **spinal surgery** or a spinal injury and requires skilled transfer and movement support
- The patient has a **neurological condition** (Parkinson's, MS, ALS) that requires specific handling techniques
- The patient is **bariatric** and needs trained safe-handling for hygiene and mobility
- The patient has had a **major surgery** with complex recovery requirements
- The patient is **post-ICU** with high physical dependency

For everyday daily-attendant needs (elderly support, post-recovery routine care, day/night/live-in flexibility), [Home Attendant Services](/home-attendant-services-bangalore/) is the better fit.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

### **How is a trained attendant different from a home attendant?**

A [home attendant](/home-attendant-services-bangalore/) handles general daily care — elderly support, post-recovery, routine personal care, with flexible shift coverage. A trained attendant is specifically for high-dependency situations — bedridden, post-spinal, neurological, or bariatric patients — where skilled physical handling (transfers, repositioning, pressure-sore prevention) is essential.

### **Do trained attendants help with bedridden patients?**

Yes. Bedridden patient care is the most common scenario for trained attendants. They support daily needs like bathing, feeding, turning in bed, hygiene care, and maintaining comfort to prevent complications like bedsores.

### **Can a trained attendant work with a home nurse?**

Yes. For patients who need both medical procedures and skilled physical handling, we can arrange a combined nurse + trained attendant placement, or see [Home Healthcare Services](/home-healthcare-services-bangalore/) for the coordinated package.

### **How quickly can you place a trained attendant?**

We try to arrange a trained attendant at the earliest possible time, especially in urgent situations like hospital discharge or post-surgery recovery. Placement timing depends on the patient's needs and availability, but we always prioritise quick and suitable matching.

### **What if I don't like the attendant?**

If the attendant is not the right fit for the patient or family, we will help arrange a replacement so the patient gets someone they feel safe and comfortable with.

### **Are your trained attendants medically qualified?**

Trained attendants are not licensed nurses — they handle non-medical physical care with training in safe patient handling. For medical procedures (wound care, IV, injections), we place a [home nurse](/home-nursing-services-bangalore/) instead, or both for combined care.

### **What is the registration or service fee?**

A one-time service or registration fee may apply depending on the type of care, duration, and urgency. Our team clearly explains all charges in advance.

### **Can I hire a trained attendant for short-term needs?**

Yes. We provide flexible options including short-term care, post-hospital support, and long-term arrangements.
